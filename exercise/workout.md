> workout
- Exercise with correct posture
- Focus on isolating the target muscle you want to grow
- Combine compound movements (e.g. squats, deadlifts) with isolation exercises
- sleep well
    - Between 10 PM and 2 AM (crucial for hormone balance and recovery)
    - Aim for 7–8 hours of quality sleep daily
